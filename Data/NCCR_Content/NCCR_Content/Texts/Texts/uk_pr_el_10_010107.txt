Press Release from Party: LibDem

Public health time bomb could bankrupt NHS says Lamb

Sun, 11 Apr 2010

The report is right to highlight the massive challenges ahead in getting better value in the NHS," said the Liberal Democrat Shadow Health Secretary.

Commenting on The Kings Fund report released today looking at the future of the NHS in light of growing health inequalities, Norman Lamb said:

The report is right to highlight the massive challenges ahead in getting better value in the NHS.

If we dont tackle the public health time bomb that faces us and take steps to invest in preventative ill health measures, we could bankrupt the NHS.
