Press Release from Party: UKIP

UK taxpayers foot EU maternity care bill

Monday, 29th March 2010

The NHS paid a 2m bill last year for foreign immigrants to go back home and give birth in their own countries, thanks to an EU scheme.

The outrageous EU policy means the British taxpayer must cough up so that immigrants - who may have been working in Britain for less than a year - can use maternity wards in their own country.

Matthew Elliott, from the TaxPayers Alliance, said: The NHS is meant to protect the health of people in this country, not finance health tourism around Europe.
