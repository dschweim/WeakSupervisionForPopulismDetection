Press Release from Party: LibDem

Governments dirty secret on Heathrow exposed says Kramer

Tue, 23 Mar 2010

Its time the Government came clean and scrapped plans for a third runway altogether," said the Liberal Democrat MP for Richmond.

Commenting on the Governments admission that a third runway at Heathrow will not benefit the UK as much as it had previously claimed, Susan Kramer, who leads the partys campaign against a third runway said:

The Governments dirty secret on Heathrow has now been exposed.
 
Ministers key argument for Heathrow expansion was that it would benefit the UK economy, but this is demonstrably false.
 
Its time the Government came clean and scrapped plans for a third runway altogether.
